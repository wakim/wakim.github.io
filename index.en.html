  • Agile Planning Poker

    My first Android app, published on Google Play Store, result of the starting my study on mobile area and Android Platform for my Graduate Thesis.

    The app provides some decks, including a customizable, for Planning Poker sessions. With animations that make the usage more interesting.

  • Distributed Planning Poker

    Evolution of Agile Planning Poker, using the Play Games® platform from Google® to allow the integration and communication between Planning Poker session peers from Distributed Teams.

    This app facilitates the realization of Planning Poker sessions between members of a Distributed Team, no matter the location of the peers.

  • Wear Planning Poker

    Extension of Agile Planning Poker, on Wearable platform. Integrates optionally with Agile Planning Poker on your Handheld (Smartphone or Tablet) to synchronize state.

    This app provides a set of decks for Planning Poker sessions, adapted to Wearable Platform.

    When connected with Agile Planning Poker on Handheld, it’s possible to mirror the state between apps (colors and selection).
